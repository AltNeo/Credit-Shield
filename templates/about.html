About CreditShield

Our Mission

CreditShield is designed to provide financial analysts with a reliable and efficient tool for assessing credit risk. Our mission is to help financial institutions make informed lending decisions by providing a comprehensive risk assessment framework.

Our Technology

The CreditShield platform uses sophisticated machine learning algorithms to predict credit risk based on various borrower and loan characteristics. The models are trained on extensive datasets and continuously refined to ensure high accuracy.

Risk Assessment Methodology

Our risk assessment methodology takes into account multiple factors:

  • Credit Score: The borrower's FICO score range
  • Debt-to-Income Ratio: The proportion of income that goes toward debt payments
  • Employment History: The stability and length of employment
  • Delinquency History: Past payment behavior
  • Loan Characteristics: The amount, term, and purpose of the loan

Grading System

CreditShield uses a comprehensive A-G grading system to categorize credit risk:

A
Very Low Credit Risk (Score: 90-100)
B
Low Credit Risk (Score: 80-89)
C
Moderate Credit Risk (Score: 70-79)
D
Average Credit Risk (Score: 60-69)
E
Elevated Credit Risk (Score: 50-59)
F
High Credit Risk (Score: 40-49)
G
Very High Credit Risk (Score: <40)
